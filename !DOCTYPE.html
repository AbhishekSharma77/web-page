Problem Statement

SmartPredict:

Predictive Maintenance Using Machine Learning

What

The problem is the need to implement a predictive maintenance system using machine learning to analyze historical sensor data from machinery in a manufacturing facility. The specific challenge is to predict equipment failures before they occur, allowing for scheduled maintenance that reduces downtime and avoids costly repairs.

Where

This problem is occurring in manufacturing facilities where machinery plays a critical role in the production process. It impacts maintenance departments, production lines, and operations teams that rely on the smooth functioning of equipment to maintain productivity.

When

The urgency of addressing this problem is ongoing, with immediate attention needed as machinery ages and the risk of unexpected breakdowns increases. Key timelines include planned maintenance schedules, production cycles, and critical periods such as before a product launch or peak production times.

Why

Solving this problem is crucial to reducing unplanned downtime, minimizing repair costs, and ensuring consistent production quality. Failure to implement predictive maintenance can lead to unexpected machinery breakdowns, resulting in production delays, increased operational costs, and a potential loss of revenue.
